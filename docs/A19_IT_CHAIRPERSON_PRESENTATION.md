## EXPORT NOTES

To produce the final 16:9 PDF from this deck:

1. **Google Slides / PowerPoint:** create a 16:9 master with Lions Blue
   header bar, gold accent line, district + LCI logos top-left/top-right,
   "We Serve — Powered by Technology" footer. Apply to all 16 slides.
2. **Image grid:** insert a 2×3 image placeholder grid on the right 60% of
   each slide. Replace each placeholder (Image 1–6) with district photos.
3. **Typography:** Headings 32–40 pt bold, bullets 18–22 pt regular,
   footer 10 pt. Use a single sans-serif family.
4. **Export:** *File → Download → PDF Document (.pdf)* with
   *"Best for printing"* / 300 DPI.
5. **Brand check:** verify every slide carries both logos, the footer,
   and exactly six images before submission.

This deck mirrors the A-19 judging rubric: roles & responsibilities,
knowledge of Lionism / Lion Portal / LLC, district IT goals, digital
development (e-directory, website, e-clubhouse, multimedia), social
media promotion, and achievements.
