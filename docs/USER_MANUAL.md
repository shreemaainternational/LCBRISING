# User Manual — Lions CRM

District 3232-F1 · Vadodara, India.

> The CRM is your dashboard. You never need to log into the Lions
> portal directly — everything authorized for you appears here.

## Logging in

1. Open the CRM (`https://YOUR_DOMAIN/`) and click **Login with Lions**.
2. You'll be redirected to the official Lions login. Sign in with your
   member credentials and MFA.
3. The CRM receives the secure token and shows your personalized
   dashboard.

## Your dashboard

- **Members** — your club roster and (if you're a higher officer) the
  zone, region, district, or full federation directory.
- **Clubs & districts** — drilldowns scoped to whatever you're
  authorized to see.
- **Events & attendance** — RSVP, mark attendance (QR or manual),
  download certificates.
- **Reports** — membership trend, retention, attendance rate, service
  hours. Export to PDF/Excel/CSV.
- **Notifications** — birthday wishes, meeting reminders, officer
  appointments, training nudges — all automated.

## Common tasks

### Update your profile
Profile → edit your phone, WhatsApp, birthday, avatar. Save changes
sync back to the central record on the next refresh.

### RSVP to an event
Events → pick an event → choose Yes / No / Maybe. You'll receive
WhatsApp + email reminders 24h before.

### Mark attendance (officers)
At the meeting, open Events → the event → **QR check-in** and let
members scan. Alternatively, mark manually from the attendee list.

### Search the directory
Members → use the search bar. Filter by club, district, or status.

### Transfer a member to another club
Members → pick the member → **Transfer**. Requires Club President or
higher. The change is audited.

## Permissions in plain English

- **Members**: see your club. Update your own profile.
- **Club officers**: manage your club's roster, events, attendance.
- **Zone / Region chairpersons**: read across your zone/region.
- **District Governor**: full read+write across the district. Create
  clubs, grant awards, approve transfers between clubs.
- **Multiple District / International Admins**: federation-wide
  controls, integrations, RBAC.

## Privacy

- We never store your Lions password — the CRM only sees the OAuth
  token issued after you log in.
- You can revoke access anytime from your Lions account settings.
- Audit logs track every privileged action; you can request your
  log via your District Governor.
